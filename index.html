About the Artist

Krissha Pearl Dela Peña

Krissha Pearl Dela Peña is a contemporary individual painting artist known for her vibrant abstract works. She is driven by the desire to inspire other young artists to confidently pursue their hobbies and creative passions. Through her art, she explores meaningful themes such as nature, human emotion, and the urban landscape bringing color, energy, and deeper reflection to every piece she creates.

Krissha Pearl Dela Peña’s artworks have been exhibited in Santo Niño, South Cotabato, featuring the theme “Smart Kabataan 2025.” She also joined an art contest organized by World Vision in Isulan, Sultan Kudarat, under the theme “Pinta Kusay,” where she proudly earned 2nd place in 2025.

“Her journey reminds young artists that a simple hobby can grow into a powerful inspiration.”
